Karima Girls' High School was started in 1969 by the Anglican Church on a (13) thirteen-acre piece of land donated by a white Settler and 1st speaker of the Kenya Legislative Assembly, Sir Humphrey Slade.19 Jun 2022

a trip to Egerton University

One hundred and sixty Agriculture students from the 'ambitious' Karima Girls High School visited Egerton University from 24-26 May 2022 on a learning tour. Five of the teachers accompanying them, including their Deputy Headteacher Josephine Mugwe, were Egerton University Alumni.

Location

Karima Girls High School is a National school and is located in the Kinangop constituency in North Kinangop near the Aberdares. It is part of the Nyandarua distict in the Central province of Kenya. It is a Girls Only Boarding school.
